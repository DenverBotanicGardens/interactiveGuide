Submitting Tissue Sample Data

Submitting data for tissue samples differs when collecting 1 sample per voucher (floristics) VS. may samples per population (population genetics).

Floristics

Tissue samples collected in conjunction with floristics projects can be batch uploaded into the Research Database along with their corresponding specimen data. Include tissue information in the same Spreadsheet Template used for Voucher Collections.

Save your spreadsheets here: Q:\Research\All_Herbaria\CollectionsDataInProgress.
Use the naming convention YYYYMMDD_FirstInitialLastName_ProjectName_Vouchers

Once complete, contact the Database Associate for review and upload. Data will be uploaded to SEINet and the Research Database.

Population Genetics

Tissue samples collected for population genetics studies, must be submitted one population (workID) at a time. This can be done using a web form if 29 or fewer individual plants were sampled. If 30 or more were sampled, complete and submit a CSV form to upload the data.

Save CSV forms here: Q:\Research\MySQL_database\DataUploads\UploadedDataFiles\BiologicalAssets if you upload yourself, or Q:\Research\MySQL_database\DataUploads\ToBeUploaded\BiologicalAssets if you would like the Database Associate to upload. Use the naming convention YYYYMMDD_FirstInitialLastName_ProjectName_Tissue
