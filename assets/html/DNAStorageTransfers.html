Storing and Transferring DNA Samples

DNA sample vials should be labelled and stored in labelled vial trays.

Samples that are currently being used for research can be stored in the lab freezer (-20°C).

Samples not currently in use should be kept for long term storage in the -80°C freezer that is located in the Horticulture Head-House. Contact your supervisor for access.
