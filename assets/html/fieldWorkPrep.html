Preparing for Field Work

It is important to prepare for field work ahead of time.

Be sure to reserve and bring all of the appropriate equipment.

Also be sure to print out and bring important required documents.

Inform the Research Coordinator ahead of time about where you are going, so they can provide you with the location and information for the nearest Hospital and/or Urgent Care Center.
