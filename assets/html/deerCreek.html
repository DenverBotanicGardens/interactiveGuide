Deer Creek Restoration and Ecological Monitoring





Vegetation

Each year 18 transects along Deer Creek (Chatfield Farms/ Hildebrand Ranch/ Jefferson County Open Space) are monitored, measuring understory percent cover, soil moisture, tree canopy cover, and plant species richness. Plant specimen vouchers are also made and deposited in the herbarium.
