Submitting Fungal Specimen Data

Data should be transcribed from mycology cards into a spreadsheet template for bulk upload.

Volunteers submitting data should fill out the Template Spreadsheet for Fungal Specimen Data to submit data for specimens.

Save your spreadsheets here: TEAMS>BiodiversityCollections-General/CollectionsDataInProgress.
Use the naming convention YYYYMMDD_FirstInitialLastName_ProjectName_Vouchers

Once complete, contact the Database Associate for review and upload. Data will be uploaded to SEINet and in some cases the Research Database.
