DNA

Denver Botanic Gardens extracts and preserves DNA samples. In many cases DNA is extracted from tissue samples, and in some cases DNA is extracted from an herbarium specimen.

DNA sample data and extraction data is stored in the Research Database. DNA sample data is also published on GGBN and can be loaned or sent to scientists in outside institutions.

DNA samples are stored on site in the Denver Botanic Gardens Biorepository, either in the -80 degrees freezer C or the lab freezer.
