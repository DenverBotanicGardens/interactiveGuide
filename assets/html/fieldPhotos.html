Taking Photos in the Field

Capturing images of field work is an important and valuable part of our work. It is important that these images are useful for a number of purposes and remain so well into the future.

Please review the protocols and procedures before setting out into the field and taking photos.

It is important to follow the protocols for taking pictures as well as processing the images so that the correct metadata can be embedded.
