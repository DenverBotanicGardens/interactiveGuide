Datasets

The Denver Botanic Gardens is committed to making data openly available.

This is often achieved through publishing well curated datasets and linking them with specimens, literature, or other assets when applicable.

Datasets are most often published on the Global Biodiversity Information Facility (GBIF)), as this is a globally accessible platform and is a widely used resource.
